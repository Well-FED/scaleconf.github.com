Workshops

With such a strong technically focused conference, we have decided to run some free workshops alongside the conference. The workshops are informal hackathon-style workshops where the engineers from various companies will be on hand to walk you through getting started on their products.

If you are interested in attending a workshop, please get your (free) tickets from the purchase page.

Cloud Essentials Workshop presented by RSAWEB

Session 1: 09:00 - 12:00
Session 2: 13:00 - 16:00

* sessions 1 and 2 cover the same content

Venue: RSAWeb offices, 123 Hope Street, Gardens

This workshop will give you hands on experience getting intimate with Cloud Computing and how to build an app & deploy it to the Cloud. Engineers from local Cloud provider, RSAWEB will show you how its done. During the workshop you will learn about various cloud options that available and have the opportunity to experiment and ask questions. This is a practical workshop intended for devs and engineers.


What topics will be worked through:

  1. Cloud overview explaining the cloud. What is out there? What is best for me?
  2. How, when and where to launch your app in the Cloud.
  3. How to provision, stop and start servers via an API.
  4. Build a small app for demo purposes.
  5. You've heard of Puppet or Chef? Let us show you how they can save you hours of time through automation.
  6. Cloud provisioning tips and tricks.
  7. Why CDN and Object Storage? How and when to use them.

This is a free workshop not to be missed. Food will be provided and each attendees will get free cloud resources to play with during and after the workshop. Just bring your laptop, we have installed Fibre at the venue, so get ready for speed and knowledge overload.
