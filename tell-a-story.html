If you want to take the bull by the horns and share your experience, please take some time to to put together a quick email with some details about what you would like to talk about. Once done, please make sure it reaches us at podium@scaleconf.org

whats in it for you

Firstly, you get the awesome prize of making South Africa a better place! By spreading your experiences you will be helping a lot of people think and approach problems differently than they might have previously. You might help expose new technologies, or maybe a new approach that hasn't been on their radar. It's also an opportunity to showcase yourself in the community, giving yourself and your company prestige in a fast growing market.
