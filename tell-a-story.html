Speakers

If you have an experience or idea you’d like to share, send a brief email with the details of your topic to info@scaleconf.org.

We’re especially interested in hearing about:

  • How you engineered your service to scale well.
  • How your company deals with scaling issues in a clever or interesting way.
  • A new way of looking at problems, and how you use it to improve your service.

Perks

Join us in driving the burgeoning tech scene in South Africa! Your experiences will help people in our young startup industry to approach problems in new and interesting ways. It’s also an opportunity to showcase yourself in the community, giving yourself and your company exposure in a fast growing market.
