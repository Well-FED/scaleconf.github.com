Speaker submissions for ScaleConf 2016 are now open!

ScaleConf will take place over two days in March 2016, in Cape Town, South Africa. It is a 300-person, single-track conference, focusing on scalability and high availability on the internet today, the challenges faced when growing a company or service, and the technologies and methodologies we can use to mitigate these problems.

We are currently accepting proposals for talks. If you would like to give a presentation, please submit your proposal using this form. The presentation slots will be 45 minutes long, including questions. The presentations will be in English.

The deadline for talk proposals is January 2016.
