Speaker submissions for ScaleConf 2016 are now closed!

ScaleConf will take place on the 10th and 11th of March, in Cape Town, South Africa. It is a 300-person, single-track conference, focusing on scalability and high availability on the internet today, the challenges faced when growing a company or service, and the technologies and methodologies we can use to mitigate these problems.
