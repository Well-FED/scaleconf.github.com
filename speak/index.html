Speaker submissions for ScaleConf 2016 are now open!

ScaleConf will take place in March 2016, in Cape Town, South Africa. It is a 300-person, single-track conference, focusing on scalability and high availability on the internet today, the challenges faced when growing a company or service, and the technologies and methodologies we can use to mitigate these problems.

There will be one day of business-oriented scaling talks, followed by two days of our usual technical talks. The Business of Scale day focuses on the issues leaders and their teams face when growing a technology-driven organisation. You'll get to hear from those who've faced the challenges of building engineering teams, growing healthy customer acquisition strategies, maintaining inspiring working environments, balancing customer demands and technical debt, dealing with exit opportunities and more.

We are currently accepting proposals for talks. If you would like to give a presentation, please submit your proposal using this form. The presentation slots will be 45 minutes long, including questions. The presentations will be in English.

The deadline for talk proposals is January 2016.
