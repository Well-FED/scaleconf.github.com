Speaker submissions for ScaleConf 2015 are now open!

ScaleConf will take place on the 4th to 6th of March, in Cape Town, South Africa. It is a 300-person, single-track conference, focusing on scalability and high availability on the internet today, the challenges faced when growing a company or service, and the technologies and methodologies we can use to mitigate these problems. There will be one day of business oriented scaling talks, followed by two days of our usual technical talks. Then on the following weekend, full- or half-day workshops on the 7th and 8th. Tickets will be available for the first day only, second two days only and the entire conference.

We are currently accepting proposals for talks. If you would like to give a presentation, please submit your proposal using this form. The presentation slots will be 45 minutes long, including questions. The presentations will be in English.

The deadline for talk proposals is the 12th of January. Accepted presenters will be notified by no later than the 31st of January, although the earlier you submit your proposal, the earlier we can accept your talk.

Submit a talk now

In addition, if you wish to hold a workshop on a particular skill, technology, or product, please submit an outline to submit@scaleconf.org. The deadline for workshop submissions is the 31st of January. Workshops can be free, or paid-for.
