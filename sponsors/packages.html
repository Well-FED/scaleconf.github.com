Sponsorship packages

ScaleConf is organised by volunteers, but there are still huge expenses when it comes to putting together a successful conference. Sponsorship helps us to keep ticket prices affordable.

We've structured our sponsorship to make sure that you can build up the benefits in a way that makes sense for your company.

If you want to take us up on one of the options then please send an email to info@scaleconf.org.

Baseline

Our starting point for sponsorship – the baseline package – gives you exposure and presence at the conference.

Benefits:
  • Company name or logo in attendee emails
  • Company name in press releases
  • Company logo on website footer
  • Company bio on website sponsors page
  • Two conference tickets
  • 15% off additional corporate tickets (maximum 5)
Costs:
  • R20,000

Level up and extend the baseline sponsorship

Baseline sponsors can tailor their package with a specific goal in mind by adding any of the extra packages below on top of baseline. These extra packages add focus to the sponsorship and help with recruiting talent, or raising awareness of their brand / product.

Spotlight

Put your company in the spotlight, and be seen at the conference.

Benefits:
  • Prominent placement and larger logo wherever used
  • Company Logo on the back of the t-shirt (given to all attendees)
  • Standing banner in main hall (to be provided, no larger than 3m x 2m)
  • Logo on Lanyard (given to all attendees)
Additional Cost (on top of Baseline):
  • R20,000
This package is limited to three sponsors.

Presence

Bring some of your engineers in to talk shop with our attendees.

Benefits:
  • Area at the back of the hall (3m x 3m)
  • Three extra conference tickets
Additional Cost (on top of Baseline)
  • R20,000
Space is the limiting factor here, so we will only be able to accommodate four spots at the venue.

Coffee

Our attendees love coffee, and they love our coffee sponsors.

Benefits:
  • Coffee sponsor designation for the day
  • Mention and Logo on the back of lanyard inserts
  • Mention during announcements
Cost: sold out

Events

We like to keep everyone socialising and networking, so on each evening of the conference, we will be giving sponsors the opportunity to host events, and meet and network with the attendees.

Benefits:
  • Official event sponsor
  • Host attendees for dinner, and/or drinks.
Additional Cost (on top of Baseline): R6,000 and the tab.
We can also manage and organize the event for you, just let us know.

Adopt a Student

Cape Town is full of smart but penniless computer science students wanting to experience and learn from events like this. It is also such a great opportunity for them to meet engineers from your company, ask questions, and get involved.

Cost: R1,100
