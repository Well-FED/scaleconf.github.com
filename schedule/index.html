Conference Schedule

We're pleased to announce that the conference schedule is extended for 2015! This years conference will run over three days, with a more business of scale oriented day running first on the Wednesday. More details of the schedule for the first day will be released soon. Tickets for the technical talks can be purchased separately if you do not wish to attend the Business of Scale day.

The Business of Scale day focuses on the issues leaders and their teams face when growing a technology-driven organisation. You'll get to hear from those who've faced the challenges of building engineering teams, growing healthy customer acquisition strategies, maintaining inspiring working environments, balancing customer demands and technical debt, dealing with exit opportunities and more.

The tried and tested formula will be repeated for the second two days, with a total of 12 x 45 minute talks plus regular breaks to keep the legs from falling asleep and awesome lunches to feed the mind and body.

Finally, we will have a weekend of full- or half-day workshops.

There will be three social events starting with the Wednesday evening with a sponsored drinks event in the city. Then similar events on the Thursday and Friday nights. These are a chance to socialise and network with the speakers, organisers, and fellow conference attendees.
