Conference Schedule

ScaleConf 2016 runs over two days packed with presentations from local and international experts on the technologies, problems and solutions that they have encountered in building sites and services that can handle the rigours of today's internet.

The two days follow our usual formula of 12 x 45 minute talks plus regular breaks to keep the legs from falling asleep and awesome lunches to feed the mind and body.

Each evening we will have a social event, to give you a chance to socialise and network with speakers, organisers, and your fellow conference attendees.
