Sponsorship Packages

Sponsorship helps to keep our ticket prices low, as ScaleConf is organized by volunteers. If you'd like to sponsor, but none of these options suit you, get in touch with us at info@scaleconf.org for more information.

As well as conference sponsorships, there are a number of events around the conference which you may like to sponsor, either for exposure, or for networking purposes.

Conference

/bin/ (Gold)

Our premier sponsorship package is aimed at companies that would like the focus to be on their brand for the majority of the conference. This sponsorship is ideal for companies wishing to advertise their product, and raise awareness of their company offerings.

In this package:

  • Banner at venue (to be provided)
  • Prominent logo on event t-shirts
  • Prominent logo on website landing page
  • Logo and paragraph on sponsors page
  • Mention on all outgoing press releases
  • Eight event passes

2 AvailableCost: R50 000

/usr/bin/ (Silver)

Our second tier sponsorship package is for companies that wish to have a presence at the conference, but not take the spotlight. This package is great for companies looking to raise awareness of their brand, seek out some potential talent, and grow their network presence in South Africa.

In this package:

  • Logo on event t-shirts
  • Prominent logo on website landing page
  • Logo and paragraph on sponsors page
  • Mention on all outgoing press releases
  • Five event passes

4 AvailableCost: R35 000

/usr/local/bin/ (Bronze)

This sponsorship package is aimed at local companies and startups who wish to make a contribution in return for some smaller branding opportunities.

In this package:

  • Logo on website landing page
  • Logo and paragraph on sponsors page
  • Two event passes

6 AvailableCost: R15 000




Event

Pre-conference drinks

The night before the conference we like to have a small mixup where attendees get a chance to socialize and network. It builds up some excitement and gives everyone a chance to break out of their work week.

In this package:

  • Credit on the event invite
  • A mention on the first day
  • Small logo and paragraph on sponsors page

1 AvailableCost: R6 000

Dinners

We would like to have some small dinners on one of the conference nights, where people can meet people they may otherwise not have met. Each dinner will be about 12-16 people, and this gives a company an excellent opportunity to network with potential hires and other people in the industry.

In this package:

  • Credit on the event invite
  • Access to conference attendees for networking purposes

6 AvailableCost: R5 000

Closing night drinks and pizza

After the conference, a lot of people stay to mingle and talk about what they've heard over a beer and some pizza.

In this package:

  • Credit on the event invite
  • A mention on the second day
  • Small logo and paragraph on sponsors page

1 AvailableCost: R8 000
